天气盒子 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 / Effective date: 2026-05-19 · 开发者 / Developer: Jiangshan Wang

1. 概述 Overview

天气盒子（“本应用”）是一款极简天气预报应用。我们高度重视你的隐私，仅为“向你展示天气”这一核心功能处理必要的最少信息。本政策说明本应用如何处理信息。

“天气盒子” (the “App”) is a minimalist weather app. We process only the minimum information necessary to show you the weather. This policy explains how the App handles information.

2. 我们收集与使用的数据 Data We Collect & Use

  • 大致位置 Approximate location：仅当你主动授予“使用期间”定位权限时，本应用会获取你的近似经纬度，并将其发送给天气数据服务商 Open-Meteo（open-meteo.com）以获取你所在位置的天气。该位置信息仅用于该次天气查询，不会被本应用上传到我们自有的服务器、不会与你的身份关联、不会用于广告或跨应用追踪。你可以拒绝授权并改为手动搜索城市，本应用同样完整可用。
    Only if you grant “When In Use” location permission, the App obtains your approximate coordinates and sends them to the weather provider Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to fetch local weather. This is used solely for that weather request, is not uploaded to any server we operate, is not linked to your identity, and is never used for advertising or cross-app tracking. You may decline and search cities manually instead.
  • 你添加的城市与应用设置 Your saved cities & settings：你搜索/添加的城市名称与坐标仅保存在你的设备本地，不会离开设备、不会被我们收集或上传。
    City names/coordinates you add are stored only locally on your device and never leave the device.
  • 无账号 No account：本应用没有注册或登录，不收集姓名、邮箱、电话或任何账户信息。
    No sign-up or login; we do not collect name, email, phone, or any account data.

3. 权限 Permissions

定位（使用期间）Location (When In Use)：用于自动显示你当前所在城市的天气。此权限为可选；不授予时可手动搜索添加城市，功能不受限。这与应用内的权限用途说明一致。
Used to automatically show weather for your current city. Optional — the App is fully functional without it via manual city search.

4. 第三方 Third Parties

本应用使用 Open-Meteo（https://open-meteo.com）作为天气与地理编码数据来源。向其发起请求时会传递你查询所在地的经纬度（来自定位或你选择的城市）。我们建议你参阅 Open-Meteo 的服务条款与隐私说明。除此之外，本应用不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。
The App uses Open-Meteo (https://open-meteo.com) for weather and geocoding data; requests include the coordinates of the location being queried. The App integrates no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

5. 数据保留与删除 Retention & Deletion

位置信息仅在发起天气请求时即时使用，本应用不对其做持久化保留。你保存的城市与设置存储于设备本地，卸载本应用即删除全部本地数据；你也可在应用内删除已添加的城市。
Location is used transiently per request and not retained by the App. Locally saved cities/settings are removed when you uninstall the App; you can also delete added cities in-app.

6. 儿童 Children

本应用面向一般用户，不专门面向儿童，且不收集可识别个人身份的信息。
The App is intended for a general audience, is not directed to children, and collects no personally identifiable information.
